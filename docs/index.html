Gateway to Legend

A Puzzle-RPG hybrid game. The core gameplay involves solving puzzles, collecting money, and defeating bosses.

This is, of course, nothing new. However, integrated directly into the core of the game is the ability to create your own adventures through the map-pack feature!
Map-packs are sets of maps, save files, spritesheets, and scripts that allow deep customization of user-generated experiences. Creating maps is easy with the map-pack toolchain program, included in the download.

In Gateway to Legend, you:

  • Fight enemies
  • Defeat bosses
  • Solve puzzles
  • Upgrade your arsenal of techniques
  • Make your own experiences (more information here)
